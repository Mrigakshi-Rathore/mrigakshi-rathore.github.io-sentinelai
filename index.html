How to Spot Misinformation

Learn to identify common manipulation techniques used in deceptive content

Emotional Manipulation

Content that uses strong emotional language to bypass critical thinking and provoke reactions

Lack of Credible Sources

Claims without verifiable evidence, references, or authoritative sources

Clickbait Headlines

Sensational or exaggerated titles that don't match the actual content quality

Repetition Tactics

Repeating false claims multiple times to create false sense of credibility

Always verify information from multiple reliable sources before sharing
